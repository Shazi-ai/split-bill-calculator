How to Use Split Bill Calculator

Split Evenly

Use this option when everyone is paying an equal share of the bill:

  1. Enter the total bill amount
  2. Enter the tax percentage (pre-filled with 8.5%)
  3. Select a tip percentage or enter a custom amount
  4. Enter the number of people sharing the bill
  5. Click Calculate to see each person's share

Split by Items

Use this option when people are paying for specific items:

  1. Add items with their prices
  2. Assign each item to a specific person or mark as shared
  3. Add the tax percentage
  4. Select a tip percentage
  5. Enter the number of people sharing the bill
  6. Click Calculate to see each person's share

Additional Features

  • Toggle between light and dark mode using the sun/moon icon
  • Access settings to customize the app
  • Share your results with others
